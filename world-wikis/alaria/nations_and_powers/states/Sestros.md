## Sestros

Tags: state

### Government & Peoples

**Government Type**: Absolute monarchy

**Primary Inhabitants**: Shailin

**Population**: 100,000

**Stability**: Very stable

**Core Values**: Glory, the graces of their god Talressses, do as your told, blind faith

- The king of Sestros is believed to have a direct link with their god Talresses.
- Believe magic is horrible, and is thus forbidden (Talresses' influence).
- Very warm towards outsiders.
- The world is very authoritarian, though they do not feel it. It is rude to ask questions.


### Economy

**Size**: Medium

**Tech**: Undeveloped

**Primary Exports**: Silks, fine textiles

**Primary Imports**: Technology

- Sestros boasts the finest textile production of anywhere in the world, attracting wealthy merchants and nobility form all over to purshase form their fine selection.
- Most people work as ranchers, herding sheep and other domestic animals across the plains.


### Military

**Size**: Medium

**Quality**: Low

**Enrollment**: Mandatory

- The whisperings of Talresses encourage the Shailin to always hold onto a standing army. Why they must do this, they are unclear, but they do not ask. 


### Geography

**Location**: Western Ve, south of Edari, west of Shyona and the Hills of Dolor

**Biomes**: Savannah plains (central), Red Desert (north), forest (south)

**Named Entities**: Red Desert, Shepherds' Stones (northern mountains), Tukiwood (southern forest), Glory River

**Rivers & Lakes**: Glory River (central, flows east toward Shyona)

**Adjacent Waters**: Nuin Bay (southwest coast)


### Political Geography

**Capital**: Mu (southwest coast, on Nuin Bay)

**Major Towns (Northern Route)**: Eidu, Dangbanr, Kanasra, Hindi Kot, Nao Goth

**Major Towns (Central Route)**: Lolani, Panjizai, Ujato, Phuklean, Wadala Sindhuan, Banda Lamman, Karborai, Jhun Tar, Bel, Wariyah, Dangara, Kothian, Basti Wahni

**Major Towns (Western)**: Philoki, Chirianwala, Kohali, Goth Kilch


### Primary Conflicts

- As the Sestros military grows, Sestros' neighbors grow wary. They wonder if war is coming.
- Innovation slows, and scholarship remains frozen in the past, as asking questions is fronwned upon, and new ideas are rare.
- The Shailin are being deluded by a prideful daemon.
