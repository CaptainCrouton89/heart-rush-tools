## Denvil Iron Claw

Tags: npc, sharabha

A vigilante gone rogue Sharabha. Is a massive lion, beautiful, ragged mane, dozens of scars, and a huge iron claw mounted to his right arm, forged by the Amverela before he had gone rogue, then repaired and upgraded by the Nydor. He can more completely transform into a lion form.

Is a very powerful leader, and has a sizeable following who help him in his goals.
