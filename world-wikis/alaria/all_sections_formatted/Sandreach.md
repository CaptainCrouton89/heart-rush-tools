## Dustforge

Tags: citystate

The city-state is built around a large oasis, where underground aquifers provide a source of water for the residents and the mushrooms that are so integral to the local economy. The mushrooms grown in Dustforge are highly prized for their unique flavors and medicinal properties

Largely underground, protected from the absolutely brutal windstorms that blast the entire luquihn desert. 

Dustforged weapons: They have developed sintering, for making metals that don't bend/break at high melting temperature (tungsten).
