# Faesong

## Faesong

Tags: magic

The music of Melera that runs through Alaria and the rest of the universe. Some creatures can use it to perform magic. It the music and magic of emotion. 

Rusalka, faeries, nelkie, rusalka, and nymphs can hear it and use it for their magic. It is also what bards reproduce when the perform gaeic melodies.

Somewhat related to the psywinds.
