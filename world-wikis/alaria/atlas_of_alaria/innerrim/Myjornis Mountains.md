## Myjornis Mountains

Tags: mountains, geography

A mountain range in the western Innerrim, forming the boundary between the Innerrim basin and the Jungles of Godahi to the north. The Myjornis Mountains run roughly east-west, connecting to the Cloud Mountains in the east.

### Notable Features

#### Yegnoth

A deep pit descending into the earth. See [Yegnoth](./Yegnoth.md) for details.

#### Scar

A brownish dragon that claims territory across the central passes. See [Scar](./Scar.md) for details.

#### Vykus

The elven city-state perched at a mountain peak. See [Vykus](./Vykus.md) for details.

### Water Features

#### Kos Askati

A river flowing from the Myjornis range northward into Qet Yemani territory, where the settlement of Tel Askati sits along its banks.

#### Desula Falls

Waterfalls in the southern Myjornis range, where mountain streams cascade down toward Gorath.

#### Boo Rapids

A stretch of dangerous rapids in the mountain rivers, notorious for claiming unwary travelers.
