## Crimson Coast

Tags: region, geography

### Geography

The Crimson Coast is a region marked by haunted waterways, mystical forests, and rolling hills. The River of Wights and the Pools of Tragedy hint at a dark history, while Hilda's Forest contains the mysterious Hildaneir. Several states including Tangiern, Klevnaf, and Istora control portions of this coastal territory.
